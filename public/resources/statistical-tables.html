Quick Formulas

Sample Size Calculations

Mean: n = (z*σ/ME)²
Proportion: n = p̂(1-p̂)(z*/ME)²

Standard Error

Mean: SE = σ/√n or s/√n
Proportion: SE = √[p̂(1-p̂)/n]

Confidence Intervals

Mean (σ known): x̄ ± z*(σ/√n)
Mean (σ unknown): x̄ ± t*(s/√n)
Proportion: p̂ ± z*√[p̂(1-p̂)/n]

Test Statistics

Z-test: z = (x̄ - μ₀)/(σ/√n)
T-test: t = (x̄ - μ₀)/(s/√n)
