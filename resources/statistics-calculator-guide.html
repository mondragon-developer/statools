Statistics Calculator Guide

How to Use the MDragon Statistics Calculator

Overview

The Statistics Calculator provides comprehensive descriptive statistics and visualizations for your data.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Access the Calculator

Navigate to: Calculators → Statistics

Step 2: Enter Your Data

Option A - Manual Entry:

  1. Type numbers separated by commas or spaces
  2. Example: 12, 15, 18, 20, 22, 25

Option B - Paste Data:

  1. Copy data from spreadsheet
  2. Paste into input field
  3. Calculator automatically detects format

Step 3: Calculate Results

Click "Calculate" button to see:

Measures of Central Tendency

  • Mean (x̄): Average value
  • Median: Middle value when sorted
  • Mode: Most frequent value

Measures of Spread

  • Range: Max - Min
  • Variance (s²): Average squared deviation
  • Standard Deviation (s): Square root of variance
  • IQR: Q3 - Q1 (middle 50%)

Quartiles

  • Q1: 25th percentile
  • Q2: Median (50th percentile)
  • Q3: 75th percentile

Example Usage

Data: Test scores: 65, 70, 75, 80, 85, 90, 95

Results:

  • Mean: 80
  • Median: 80
  • Std Dev: 10.8
  • Range: 30

Interpretation:

  • Average score is 80
  • Half scored above/below 80
  • Typical deviation from mean is ~11 points

Tips

✓ Remove outliers if appropriate
✓ Use at least 5-10 data points
✓ Check for data entry errors
✓ Compare mean vs median for skewness
